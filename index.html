Event Walkthrough


Our event managing team will put organize biweekly community clean ups at a decided location. Details will be posted in advanced below on the event board as well as on our social medias. Volunteers will gather up with the team at a given location where gloves and garbage bags will be provided. Items that should not be picked up will be mentioned and after formailties and pictures, volunteers can go their own ways in groups. After the two hours, volunteers will gather back up to the decided spot and the garbage collected will be gathered and disposed of accordingly. Lastly, the refreshments and snacks will be given out to all the volunteers.
